When is 2FA day?

Each year on the 2nd of February, World 2FA Day is celebrated.
The 2nd of Febuary, also known as 2nd FActor day, has been chosen because it has 2FA in the date itself: 2nd of FebruAry.
It's celebrated right after "Change your 1FA day" on the 1st of February.
02-02-2022 was the first annual 2FA Day.

What is 2FA?

2FA (or MFA) stands for 2 (or multi) factor authentication. It's an extra layer of security to add when having to login to a certain services, in addition to your password.
More information about 2FA on Wikipedia.
